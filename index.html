Account & Data Deletion

This page explains how to request deletion of your Lifestyle Coach account and associated data.

Request deletion

Submit this form to request account and data deletion:

Account & Data Deletion Request Form

If you cannot access the form, email us at tanvirabu71@gmail.com .

What data will be deleted

  • Account identifiers you provided (e.g., email, username)
  • In-app content linked to your account (e.g., goals, plans, tasks, messages)
  • Any stored preferences linked to your account

Processing time

We process deletion requests within 7 days after verifying account ownership.

Verification

We may contact you at the email you submitted to confirm ownership before deleting data.
